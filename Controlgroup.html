                    Chasing Dreams
                    by: Jonalyn Espares

                    I’m weary, tired, and worn,
                    The night feels endless, hearts are torn.
                    The path ahead is rough, unclear,
                    Yet I press on, despite the fear.

                    I’ve stumbled many times before,
                    Fallen hard, can’t take much more.
                    The weight of dreams pulls me down,
                    Yet I rise, despite the frown.
                    For in the silence of my soul,
                    A whisper calls, it makes me whole.
                    A dream that burns within my chest,
                    A vision that won't let me rest.

                    Though tired, I’ll keep the fight,
                    I’ll chase my dreams through endless night.
                    For in the depths of struggle’s grip,
                    I find the strength to never slip.

                    So I'll carry on, though shadows creep,
                    For dreams are worth the price I keep.
                    No, I won't give up, though I'm undone,
                    I’ll chase my dreams until they’re won.
